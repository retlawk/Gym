Warming up - Endurance

Endurance means doing sets with more then 15 repetitions.

Bike to the gym. At the gym use light weight and high intensity.

Calisthenics - Hypertrophy

Hypertrophy means doing sets between the 6 and 15 repetitions.

Train with the weight of your own body, average amount of rest between sets.

Key sets - Strenght

Strenght means doing sets with less then 6 repetitions.

Go heavy, do a drop set at the end. Go for 95% of max, longer resting periods between sets.

Super sets - Hypertrophy

Hypertrophy means doing sets between the 6 and 15 repetitions.

2 Exercises (sets) in succession. Train at +- 70% of your max, average amount of rest between sets.

Normal sets - Hypertrophy

Hypertrophy means doing sets between the 6 and 15 repetitions.

Train at +- 80% of your max, average amount of rest between sets.

Beastmode sets - Endurance

Endurance means doing sets with more then 15 repetitions.

All Exercises (sets) in succession. Train at +- 60% of your max, no rest between sets.
