Endurance

Endurance means doing sets with light weights for more then 15 repetitions.

Bike to the gym. At the gym use light weight and high intensity.

Calisthenics - Hypertrophy

Hypertrophy means doing sets with medior weight between the 6 and 15 repetitions.

Train with the weight of your own body, average amount of rest between sets.

Strenght

Strenght means doing sets with heavy weights with less then 6 repetitions.

Go heavy, do a drop set at the end. Go for 100% of max, longer resting periods between sets.

Super sets - Hypertrophy

Hyperthropy means doing sets with medior weight between the 6 and 15 repetitions.

2 Exercises (sets) in succession. Train at +- 70% of your max, average amount of rest between sets, the first set could be at 80% of max and the successed set at 60% of max.

Normal sets - Hypertrophy

Hypertrophy means doing sets with medior weight between the 6 and 15 repetitions.

Train at +- 80% of your max, average amount of rest between sets.
