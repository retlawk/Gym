Warming up

Come to the gym by bike to raise the temperature of the body. At the gym warm up with light weights and high intensity.

Calisthenics

Compound body weight exercises. Suitable for any fitness level and good for your posture. Do as many repetitions as you can.

Key compound exercise

The most important exercises for increasing strenght, do a drop set at the end. You should not be able to do more then 6 repetitions. When reaching plateaus, try deloading.

Super set

Good exercises to get a lot of sets in your 90 minute workout. Good for muscle size, try to train at +- 70% of your max with 6 to 12 repetitions.

Normal set

For ease or a little rest we sometimes do a normal set. train at +- 80% of your max with 6 to 12 repetitions.

Endurance hypertrophy

Super series exercise for an opposing superset. No rest between sets, train at +- 70% of your max with 6 to 12 repetitions.
