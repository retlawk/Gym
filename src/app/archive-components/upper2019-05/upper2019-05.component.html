Warming up

5 different sets in a row - 5 minutes

Warm up your upper body with low weights and a standing position. Do Rear Delt Raises, Side Lateral Raises, Standing Shoulder Press, Biceps Curls, Overhead Tricep Extensions.

Pull ups & Dips

2 supersets - 5 minutes

Pull up form: shoulder-width grip, pull your elbows to the floor & go all the way. Dip form: straighten your arms, dip below your elbows & lock your elbows at top.

Benchpress

Pyramid with drop, 5 sets - 10 minutes

Key workout, focus on weight & drop the last set. Benchpress form: medium grip, thumbs around the bar, lower the bar to your mid chest.

Incline Dumbbell & Squeeze Press

3 supersets - 10 minutes

Do the Incline Dumbbell Press at 80% of your max. When all reps are done continue with the Dumbbell Squeeze Press on 60% of your max.

Cable Fly & Low Cable Crossover

3 supersets - 10 minutes

Do the Cable Fly at 80% of your max. When all reps are done continue with the Low Cable Crossover.

Cable Row & Incline Chest

4 supersets with drop - 10 minutes

Key workout, focus on weight & drop the last set.

Lat Pull-Down & Shrugs

3 supersets - 10 minutes

Do the Lat Pull-Down at 80% of your max. Shrugs always with 20kg plates.

Wide back row & Rear Delt Raises

3 supersets - 10 minutes

Do the Wide back row at 80% of your max and the Rear Delt Raises at 60%.

3 sets - 5 minutes

Normal set, 80% of max.
